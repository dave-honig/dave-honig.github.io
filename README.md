## Featured Projects

### Olist Brazilian E-Commerce
**PostgreSQL · Tableau**

Analyzed 100,000 Brazilian e-commerce orders across nine tables to identify the operational drivers of negative customer reviews. The analysis revealed that carrier transit time — not seller quality or freight cost — is the dominant platform-wide cause of customer dissatisfaction.

[View project summary »](https://dave-honig.github.io/olist-brazilian-e-commerce)

### Top Boardgames
**Excel · Tableau**

Examined commonalities among the top-rated board games to identify patterns that could point a development team toward the right design direction.

[View project summary »](https://dave-honig.github.io/top-boardgames)

### Subscription Analytics
**PostgreSQL**

Analyzed subscription data for a fictional nonprofit providing access to global healthcare database metadata — covering revenue trends, churn rates, payment failures, and customer lifetime value. Demonstrates core SQL skills including CTEs, window functions, JOINs, aggregations, and date functions across four related tables (customers, subscriptions, payments, and support tickets).

[View project summary »](https://dave-honig.github.io/subscription-analysis)
